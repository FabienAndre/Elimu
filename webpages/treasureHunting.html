Treasure Hunter

Let's give conditionals a whirl! Karel stands on the brink of completing his quest to uncover buried treasure. The hole infront of Karel might contain contain either one, two or three beepers (Karel's version of gold). This program makes Karel retrieve the treasure without crashing. Before the program starts Karel can't know how many beepers there will be, so Karel must use conditions.
