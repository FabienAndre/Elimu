About

Karel the Robot is an elegant introduction to the world of programing. The simple syntax lets first time coders concentrate on learning core concepts and solving interesting problems instead of memorizing symbols.

Special thanks to Eric Roberts for, writing a majority of the Karel content that is used on stanfordkarel.com and for keeping Karel going all these years!

This site was created by Chris and The Karel Group: Tom, Joe and Jonathan (1) in celebration of Karel and (2) with the intent of using Karel to introduce students--hopefully from around the world--to Java Script.

If you have any questions or comments (eg I would like to donate a lot of money to The Karel Group) you can contact us by email: piech@cs.stanford.edu.
